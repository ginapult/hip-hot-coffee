Barista of the month: Alexandra Morales

Meet our Barista of the month for May, Alexandra!

What is your favorite Coffee? "I'd say my favorite drink is probably our single origin Burundi roast in a cortado."

What do you love most about working here? "The people. Hands down. We have so many awesome customers and I love chatting with them about life and putting a smile on thier faces."
